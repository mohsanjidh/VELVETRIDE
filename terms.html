Terms & Conditions

Velvet Ride Rentals provides premium chauffeur-driven vehicles for weddings, corporate travel, airport transfers, special occasions, and luxury transportation services.

By confirming a booking, the client agrees to comply with all terms, policies, and conditions stated herein. Velvet Ride Rentals reserves the right to refuse, modify, or cancel bookings in circumstances involving safety concerns, misinformation, regulatory requirements, vehicle unavailability, or force majeure events.

Vehicle images displayed on the website are representative. While every effort is made to provide the requested vehicle model, equivalent or upgraded alternatives may be offered when necessary.

Booking & Payment Policy

  • Advance booking confirmation requires payment of the half of total amount payable by the customer.
  • The remaining balance must be settled before or at the commencement of service unless otherwise agreed in writing.
  • Bookings are confirmed only after receipt of payment and issuance of confirmation from Velvet Ride Rentals.
  • Additional charges incurred during service, including extended hours, extra mileage, parking fees, toll charges, and special requests, shall be payable by the client.
  • All payments are non-transferable unless approved by management.

Fuel Policy

  • Fuel costs are included only when explicitly mentioned in the booking confirmation.
  • Any additional travel beyond the agreed itinerary may incur supplementary fuel and operational charges.
  • For custom itineraries, fuel charges will be calculated based on actual usage and communicated transparently.
  • Most of our cars are the charges are including fuel and driver for 80 km and 8 hours.

Damage Policy

For chauffeur-driven services, clients are expected to maintain reasonable care of the vehicle throughout the rental period.

Clients may be held responsible for:

  • Interior damage caused by negligence.
  • Excessive cleaning requirements resulting from spills, stains, smoking, or misconduct.
  • Damage caused by passengers to vehicle interiors, accessories, entertainment systems, or fittings.

Normal wear and tear shall not incur charges. Any damage assessment shall be documented and communicated to the client before charges are applied.

Cancellation Policy

More than 14 days before service: Full refund of booking amount, excluding payment gateway charges.

7–14 days before service: 50% of booking amount refundable.

Less than 7 days before service: Booking amount is non-refundable.

Same-day cancellation: No refund shall be applicable.

In cases of extreme weather conditions, vehicle mechanical issues, government restrictions, or force majeure events, Velvet Ride Rentals may offer rescheduling options at its discretion.

Rental Agreement

The rental agreement commences at the scheduled reporting time and concludes upon completion of the agreed service period.

The client agrees that:

  • The vehicle remains the property of its respective owner or leasing partner.
  • The chauffeur operates under Velvet Ride Rentals’ service standards.
  • Service timings, routes, and schedules shall be adhered to as reasonably as possible.

Vehicle Handover

  • Vehicles are delivered in clean, presentable, and roadworthy condition.
  • Clients are encouraged to inspect the vehicle upon arrival.
  • Any concerns must be reported immediately before commencement of service.
  • The chauffeur shall remain responsible for vehicle operation throughout the service period.

Usage Restrictions

The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Smoking or vaping inside the vehicle.
  • Consumption of illegal substances.
  • Transportation of hazardous, flammable, or prohibited materials.
  • Reckless, abusive, or unsafe behavior toward chauffeurs.
  • Any activity that may damage the vehicle or violate applicable laws.

Violation may result in immediate termination of service without refund.

Geographic Limitations

  • Services are available throughout Kerala and selected interstate destinations subject to prior approval.
  • Certain remote, restricted, or high-risk areas may not be serviceable.
  • Interstate travel must be disclosed during booking for route planning and permit compliance.

Breakdown Assistance

While all vehicles undergo routine maintenance and inspection, unforeseen mechanical issues may occasionally occur. In such circumstances, Velvet Ride Rentals will:

  • Provide immediate support and coordination.
  • Arrange a replacement vehicle whenever reasonably possible.
  • Minimize disruption to the client’s schedule.

Compensation, if any, shall be limited to the value of the affected service segment.

Driver Limitations

  • Only the assigned Velvet Ride Rentals chauffeur is authorized to operate the vehicle.
  • Self-driving is not permitted unless specifically stated in a separate agreement.
  • Chauffeurs are required to comply with all traffic regulations and safety standards.
  • Drivers may refuse instructions that violate traffic laws, safety regulations, or company policy.
